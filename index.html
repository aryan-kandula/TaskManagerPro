How to Use It
1
Add Tasks Manually
Hit + Add Task. Fill in description, due date, priority, and course. Priority is also auto-detected from the task name — quizzes and exams rank higher than labs, exercises, and attendance.
2
Import from Canvas or Google Calendar
Click Import, paste your Canvas Calendar Feed URL or Google iCal link. Tasks import and auto-refresh every 5 minutes going forward. Priority is auto-assigned intelligently.
3
Bulk-Complete Overdue Tasks
In the Past Due section, use Mark All Done or pick a date range to bulk-complete tasks you've already finished. No more tapping one by one.
4
Ask the AI Assistant
Click any Quick Question — it fires instantly. Ask things like "schedule my tasks this week" and get a real day-by-day plan with actual task names spread sensibly across the week.
5
Navigate the Week View
Use the ← → arrows to move between weeks. Click any day tile to see just that day's tasks below. Today is highlighted in blue.
6
Set a Custom Timer
In the Study Timer tab or inside any task detail panel, type any number of minutes into the custom input. Not limited to 25/50/5 presets.
