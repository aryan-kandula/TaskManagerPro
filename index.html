How to Use It
1
Add Tasks Manually
Hit + Add Task in the header. Fill in the description, due date, priority, and which course it's for. Tasks are sorted automatically by urgency.
2
Import from Canvas or Google Calendar
Click Import, paste your Canvas Calendar Feed URL or Google iCal link. Tasks will be imported and the app will auto-refresh every 5 minutes going forward.
3
Click Any Task to Open It
Tap a task card to open the full detail panel. You'll see the urgency score, time remaining, your notes, and a focus timer — all in a beautiful deep-glass view.
4
Ask the AI Assistant
Go to the AI Assistant tab and ask anything — "what should I focus on today?", "am I falling behind?", "what's due this week?" The AI reads all your tasks and responds intelligently.
5
Use the Study Timer
Go to Study Timer, pick a task from the list, and start a Pomodoro session. The animated ring tracks your time and notifies you when the session ends.
6
Check Your Week View
Switch to Week View to see tasks plotted across each day of the current week. Click any task chip to open its detail panel instantly.
